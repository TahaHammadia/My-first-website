The Covid-19 Political Game

Ruler, save your people (or your power) from Covid-19 !!

In this game, you will have the opportunity to husband the governemental reaction to Covid-19 in some country for one year. Your plan should take into consideration that your popularity should not be less than 30 % for two weeks. Otherwise, the country will flare up.

Please take into consideration that your gaming won't be saved



Please note that this game has no political agenda, but is simply a simulation of what really happens.
